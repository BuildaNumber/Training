Why Dyscalculia Training Matters

Dyscalculia affects approximately 5% of students, yet it remains significantly underdiagnosed compared to other learning disabilities. Many educators lack the specific training needed to identify and support students with mathematical learning difficulties effectively.

Our comprehensive training program equips educators, SENCOs, educational psychologists, and support staff with the knowledge and practical skills to make a real difference in the lives of students struggling with mathematics.

What You'll Gain

Deep understanding of the cognitive processes underlying mathematics learning, evidence-based assessment techniques using Numeralis, practical intervention strategies you can implement immediately, and confidence to support students across all educational stages from primary school to university level.
